About Me

Hi! My name is Nur Fatin Nasuha Binti Abdullah. I'm 21 years old. I'm kelantanese. I am the second child and the youngest of 2 siblings. I was born on 12 May 2002 at Hospital Raja Perempuan Zainab II (HRPZ II) in Kota Bharu, Kelantan and I live in Tumpat, Kelantan. My favourite colour is black, blue, beige and green. I am actually an orphan, my father passed away 11 years ago when I was 9 years old. Now I live with my mother, brother and sister-in-law.

Next, I have several hobbies such as shopping, playing badminton and traveling. All these hobbies I always do in my life. I also have some skills such as time management, problem solving and teamwork. These skills are important because it can be practiced in everyday life. I am an ambivert which means sometimes I can be an introvert and sometimes I can be an extrovert depending on the situation and circumstances.
